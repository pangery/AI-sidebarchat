Privacy Policy — AI Sidebar Chat

Last updated: May 19, 2026

AI Sidebar Chat runs in your browser. It does not operate its own backend servers and does not sell your data.

Data we access

DataPurposeSharing
Page title, URL, text excerpt Build context for AI questions Processed locally; pasted or sent only to the AI service you choose
Selected text Optional question context Same as above
Settings & optional API keys Extension preferences Stored in browser chrome.storage only

Web mode

Official AI websites load in a sidebar. You use your own accounts. Context is copied on your device (clipboard). The extension author does not receive your chats.

API mode (optional)

API requests go directly from your browser to the provider you configure. Keys stay in extension storage on your device.

What we do not do

  • No analytics or ad trackers built into the extension
  • No sale of personal data
  • No extension-owned user accounts

Third parties

AI providers (OpenAI, Google, Anthropic, etc.) have their own privacy policies for conversations on their platforms.

Deletion

Uninstall the extension or clear its data in browser extension settings.
